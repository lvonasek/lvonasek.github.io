Lubos Vonasek Programmierung

Luboš Vonásek is a standalone developer, who released an app at the end of 2016 for the Google Tango project called OpenConstructor. The project Tango was terminated in summer 2017, and there were no other technologies capable of 3D scanning on mobile phones.


XR Conjuring

I integrated the largest 3D models database Sketchfab into a mixed reality app. I ported the Sketchfab Unity integration on Android and made it work in realtime. Using Voice SDK you can search anything you want and get it mapped on your hands using Hand SDK.

Pi Launcher

This is a simple launcher supporting Android/Official Quest/SideQuest apps and games. It enhances the user experience of the Quest VR headsets.

AR Demo

Official SDK demos are hard to compile. This demo improves the original AR demo scene and extend it by automatical floor detection, drawing and cube throwing.
